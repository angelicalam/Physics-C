Can You Find the Mass-to-Charge Ratio?


Find the mass-to-charge ratio of the positively-charged particle.

The E field is in the plane of the page while the B field is perpendicular to the plane of the page.

When the B field is positive, it is directed out of the page and vice versa.

Gravitational effects are ignored.

Press the green button to release the particle from rest and press it again to reset the simulation.


This simulation is inspired by the physics concepts behind a mass spectrometer.
